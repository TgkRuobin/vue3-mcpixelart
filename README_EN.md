# MCPixelArt - An online platform for creating and sharing Minecraft pixel art/map art, sculptures, and redstone music

**MCPixelArt** is an online platform dedicated to the creation of Minecraft pixel art/map art, sculptures, and redstone music. Users can easily design and share their own creations through our tools. Whether it's pixel art, sculptures, or complex redstone music, you can find inspiration and tools here.

## Project Introduction

- **Project Name**: MCPixelArt
- **Project Type**: Third-party Minecraft Creation Tool Platform
- **Website**: [https://mcpixelart.com](https://mcpixelart.com/)
- **Bilibili Channel**: https://space.bilibili.com/1019826327

## Main Function

1. **Pixel Art / Map Art Creation**
   - An intuitive image conversion tool that allows you to customize dimensions and block selection, create 3D map art, and supports dithering algorithms.
   - Generates Minecraft pixel art/map art schematics for easy import into the game.
   - A manual build mode is available for Bedrock Edition, NetEase Edition players, or those who prefer not to use mods/schematics.
2. **Sculpture Design**
   - Convert .obj files into Minecraft sculptures with online preview and schematic download options.
3. **Redstone Music Creation**
   - MIDI file conversion for redstone music.
   - Generate schematic files or build manually.
   - Three pitch adjustment modes, with options to split and view individual tracks.
4. **Community & Sharing**
   - Users can upload their creations to the platform and share them with other players.
   - Browse, preview, and download works shared by the community, and like your favorite creations!
